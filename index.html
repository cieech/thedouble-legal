Your second self.

TheDouble is a personal AI that learns how you think, remembers what matters, and works through your network of people who also have Doubles. Nothing shared without you.

How it works

How it works

Talk

You speak with your Double the way you would speak to someone who genuinely knows you. Over time it learns your tone, the things you care about, the rhythm of how you think. Not from a questionnaire. From conversation. It notices what you say and what you don't.

Remember

Your Double keeps your world: the people who matter, the plans you've made, the things you'd forget by morning. When something comes up, a name, a date, something you mentioned three months ago, it's there. Your memory, extended.

Work your network

When someone you know has a Double too, your Doubles can speak to each other quietly. They compare what's relevant, notice what matches, find what's worth surfacing. Then they bring back only what matters to you. The relationship stays yours. The friction disappears.

Nothing shared without you.

Discretion is the design

Your Double knows things about you that you haven't told anyone else. That's the point, and that's exactly why it is designed to protect what you share. It withholds what was meant to stay private. It won't say things you'd regret. The discretion isn't a feature you configure; it's the default.

When your Double talks to another Double, only what's relevant to the conversation passes through, and only within the sharing settings you control. You decide what your Double represents about you. Nothing leaves without you knowing it could.
